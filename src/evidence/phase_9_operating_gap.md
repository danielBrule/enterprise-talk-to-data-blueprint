# Phase 9 — Operating Gap

## Status

Not applicable to current implementation.

This is not a live operated service. Phase 9 is included to make clear what would be needed to run, support and improve a Talk-to-Data capability after controlled release.

## Operating decision

No production operation is in scope.

The current repository should be treated as a discovery MVP and reference implementation, not as a service with users, support, monitoring or lifecycle ownership.

## Phase 9 intent for this implementation

Phase 9 answers one practical question:

> What would be required to operate this capability safely after release?

For this implementation, the answer is an operating gap: the technical foundations exist, but live-service processes do not.
